WELCOME TO
EMPIRE HOTEL

The perfect luxury: in one of the busiest environments for holidaymakers in the region of Durres, the Empire Hotel Durres Albania is a remarkable destination that offers hospitality in an atmosphere of unmatched classical and timeless elegance. It has its own private beach and offers countless opportunities for those who want to try a unique experience with all the colors of nature. We also strive for a sustainable luxury. We at the Empire Hotel, are committed, to deliver an exceptional guest experience with the best service while also creating unique moments and memories for our guests from all around the world.

SWIMMING POOL

The Empire Hotel’s sixth-floor roof-top open pool is a little-known gem designed to blend in with the magnificent baroque-style hotel architecture. Even if you are just a breath away from our beautiful private beach, our pool will always be a refuge and the place where you may start or end your day. Especially, after a long day of traveling or sightseeing, this summer-worthy retreat will leave you feeling cool all season long.

PRIVATE BEACH

Our beautiful private beach is situated 150 m away from the hotel. Whether you close your eyes and listen to the magnificent Adriatic Sea waves lapping the sand or feel the gentle breezes, you’re guaranteed a relaxing time in this nice environment. Terracotta-colored sunbeds and straw parasols all contribute to your comfort and relaxation
